GO GREEN BAPPA

Ganpati Bappa Morya!

The Ganesha Festival is just few days away. A great chance for nature lovers to set their hands on an eco-friendly ganpati idol…

Our Eco Friendly Ganesha Idols are made from natural clay that will dissolve in 45 mins. Our idols are painted with natural colors which are 100% safe

We all know that, after immersions on the 11th day. Visiting the beach on the next day of immersion is a pathetic sight. You see the beach full un-dissolved idol limbs, polythene bags, flowers, thermocol, plastics, and toxic chemical dyes.

This Ganesh Chaturthi, Puneites can revive the beloved nature by using Ganesh idols made of Clay, while saving the environment at the same time. We have come up with this initiative of making biodegradable Ganesh idols made from Clay and paper mache. Hand-sculpted by traditional sculptors village, the idols are painted with natural colors like turmeric for the ornaments and clothes, multani mitti for the skin color and geroo for Ganesha’s seat and eco friendly paint.

We promotes Green Living. Our vision is to create awareness and responsibility towards correcting the damage to the nature. Our innovations have led us to creating eco-friendly idols for Ganesh Chaturti Festival.
